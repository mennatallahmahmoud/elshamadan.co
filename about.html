Story Of ElShamadan

ElShamadan Company is considered one of the largest biscuits and sweet bakery companies, thanks to its various products, including biscuits filled with chocolate, plain biscuits, and other sweet baked goods. ElShamadan specializes in the production of biscuits, wafers, cakes and chocolate, and was established in 1978. ElShamadan production began in 1984 and was the most popular type of biscuit in the Egyptian market. ElShamadan also exports its products to Saudi Arabia, Lebanon and Tunisia.
